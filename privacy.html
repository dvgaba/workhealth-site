Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Workhealth ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. The Information We Collect

Workhealth is a local-first desktop application. Most of your data stays on your machine.

  • Local Settings: Your timer settings, conditions, and preferences are stored locally in the app.
  • Google Calendar Data: If you connect your Google Calendar, we retrieve your event schedule to smartly pause health reminders.

Google API Limited Use Disclosure

Workhealth's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically regarding Google Calendar data:

  • We only request read-only access (calendar.readonly and calendar.events.readonly) to check when you are in meetings.
  • We do not use this data to train generalized AI models.
  • We do not transfer or sell this data to third parties.
  • The calendar data is processed locally by the desktop application.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to provide and improve the core functionality of the Workhealth application (e.g., stopping timers when you are in a meeting).

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Since Workhealth is a local-only application, we do not store your data on external servers. All data is saved on your device.

  • To delete your data: Simply uninstall the application and clear the local application data folder.
  • To revoke Google Calendar access: You can click the "Disconnect" button within the Workhealth Integrations tab, or revoke access directly from your Google Account Permissions page.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. Your Google OAuth tokens are safely stored on your local device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or need support, please contact us at workhealth.help@gmail.com. For app issues, you can also reach us via the GitHub repository issues page.
